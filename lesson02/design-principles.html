Design Principles

Landon McConnell

Contrast

MCDM productions

The MCDM landing page does an excellent job of using contrast to draw the users attention to the important elements by using a white background with a large dark image directly above the bolded link to follow the development of their new game. The image even has a contrast between its edge, and a spotlight shining on the characters, and the title of the game.

Proximity

Armor Games

The armor games home page is a good example of proximity at work, because even though the new games and the popular games are both in columns, theres noticeably more space between them than between the columns of new games. Theres also a larger space separating those from the all games category at the bottom.

Alignment

NASA

In this image, we can see how the top image is centered, and then the image/text pairs beneath it are formatted so that either edge of their content aligns with the edges of the image above.
